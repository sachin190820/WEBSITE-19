Have questions?
How many designs do I get?
It depends on how many requests you create and how big they are! We share new work every 24-48 hours so we move through requests pretty quickly. If you keep your backlog updated and give feedback quickly, we should be able to get a lot done each month.
Why wouldn 't I just hire a full-time designer?
They 're way more expensive and harder to manage. A senior-level designer like the ones at Bitesized will easily cost you $100k+, require benefits, vacation days, etc. It adds up!
Is there a limit to how many requests I can have?
Nope. You can add as many requests as you 'd like to your backlog. We 'll keep working on them one at a time (two at a time if you bought Dinner for 2) until they 're all done. It 's best if you keep us busy and always have requests in your backlog.
What if I’m not happy with my designs?
How dare you! Just kidding. You can send it back to the kitchen and we 'll keep revising until it 's exactly what you 're looking for.
